Doctoral Thesis

This page provides information and links related to Kunio Kondo's doctoral thesis, Research on the Representation of Three-Dimensional Shapes Using Interactive Rendering, submitted to the University of Tokyo in 1988.

Doctoral Thesis, 1988

インタラクティブレンダリングによる3次元形状の表現に関する研究

Kunio Kondo received the degree of Doctor of Engineering from the University of Tokyo in March 1988 by submitting this doctoral thesis.

Thesis title:
Research on the Representation of Three-Dimensional Shapes Using Interactive Rendering
Japanese title: インタラクティブレンダリングによる3次元形状の表現に関する研究

Degree: Doctor of Engineering, The University of Tokyo, 1988

Review committee:
Main examiner: Professor Fumihiko Kimura
Examiners: Professor Hiroyuki Yoshikawa, Professor Kenjiro Suzuki, Professor Kokichi Sugihara, and Professor Shigeo Ozono
