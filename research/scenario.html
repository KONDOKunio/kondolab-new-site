Scenario Writing

Introduction to My Research Papers

This page introduces research on scenario writing methods, scenario writing support systems, and digital scrapbooks or writing-support databases for scenario production. These studies aim to support the creation, structuring, analysis, visualization, and reuse of scenario information in visual content production.

Scenario Writing Method

シナリオ執筆支援手法

A scenario is an important intermediate product that forms the foundation of visual content production. It must contain information necessary for later production processes. This research aims to digitize various kinds of information written in scenarios and to develop methods for effectively using scenario information in downstream production processes.

The research constructs scenario writing support systems that organize the writing process from initial ideas to S, M, and L plots, full plots, draft scripts, and final scripts. The results showed that even beginners with little scenario writing experience could produce scenarios of sufficient volume, and that the system could support scenario production with the level of quality required by specialists such as scenario writers.

“Name”: A Storyboard Construction Method in Manga Production

ストーリーマンガ制作のための段階的なネーム構成支援手法

This research proposes a step-by-step story construction support method for original manga production. It focuses on “name,” the storyboard-like intermediate form used in manga production, and supports story and page structure design.
