Biography

KONDO Kunio

Ph.D. in Engineering, The University of Tokyo, 1988.

Kunio KONDO is an Emeritus Professor in the School of Media Science, Tokyo University of Technology. He received his Doctor of Engineering from the University of Tokyo in 1988 and his bachelor's degree from Nagoya Institute of Technology in 1978.

He was an Associate Professor in the Department of Information and Computer Sciences, Saitama University, from 1989 to 2007. He was a Lecturer at Tokyo Polytechnic University from 1988 to 1989, and a technical staff member at Nagoya University from 1973 to 1988. He also served as a part-time lecturer at the University of Tokyo from 1991 to 2007, Aichi Prefectural University of Fine Arts and Music from 1989 to 1999, and Kyushu Institute of Design from 2002 to 2010. He has also served as a Visiting Professor at Management and Science University in Malaysia since 2014.

In 2020, he served as a Visiting Professor at Toho University in Japan, the University of Silesia in Katowice in Poland, and Brawijaya University in Indonesia. He was also a curriculum committee member of the Digital Technology Program at King Mongkut's University of Technology Thonburi from 2020 to 2023.

His research interests include computer graphics, animation, games, and interactive modelling.

He received the IPSJ Anniversary Best Paper Award in 1985, the JSGS Research Award in 1985, and the JSGS Best Paper Award in 2011. He also received the Yayasan MSU-ADADA Award for Lifetime Achievement in Digital Art and Design in 2019. He is a Fellow of IPSJ, the Information Processing Society of Japan, and a Fellow of IIEEJ, the Institute of Image Electronics Engineers of Japan.

He is the President of ADADA International and ADADA Japan. He was formerly President of the Institute of Image Electronics Engineers of Japan, President of the Society for Art and Science, Vice President of the Japan Society for Graphic Science, Chair of the Special Interest Group on Computer Graphics and CAD of the Information Processing Society of Japan, and a Board Member of the Asia Digital Art and Design Association.

略歴

近藤邦雄

コンピュータグラフィックス、アニメやゲーム制作のためのコンテンツ工学等の 研究に従事。ADADA会長, 画像電子学会会長、Visual Computing研究委員会委員長、 芸術科学会会長、情報処理学会グラフィクスとCAD研究会主査、 日本図学会副会長、ISGG理事、日本図学会デジタルモデリング研究会副委員長 などを歴任。

情報処理学会25周年記念論文賞、日本図学会賞、 関東工学教育賞、NICOGRAPH奨励賞などを受賞。 コンピュータグラフィックス、アニメ、ゲームなどの映像コンテンツ制作に 関する研究に従事している。
