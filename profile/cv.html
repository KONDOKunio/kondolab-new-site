Curriculum Vitae

This page summarizes the curriculum vitae of Professor Kunio Kondo, including education, professional appointments, visiting professorships, part-time lectureships, academic society activities, international conference activities, and related social activities.

Download Curriculum Vitae PDF

Education

  • 1978: Graduated from the Department of Mechanical Engineering, Evening Division, Nagoya Institute of Technology.
  • 1988: Doctor of Engineering, The University of Tokyo.

Professional Appointments

  • 1973: Technical Assistant and Educational Assistant, Graphic Science Laboratory, College of General Education, Nagoya University.
  • 1988: Lecturer, Department of Electronic Engineering, Tokyo Polytechnic University.
  • 1989: Associate Professor, Department of Information and Computer Sciences, Faculty of Engineering, Saitama University.
  • 2007: Professor, School of Media Science, Tokyo University of Technology.
  • 2020: Emeritus Professor, Tokyo University of Technology.

Visiting Professorships

  • 2014–present: Visiting Professor, Management and Science University, Malaysia.
  • 2020: Visiting Professor, Toho University, Japan.
  • 2020: Visiting Professor, University of Silesia in Katowice, Poland.
  • 2020: Visiting Professor, Brawijaya University, Indonesia.
  • 2021: Kanagawa Institute of Technology.
  • 2021: Kobe Design University.

JICA Short-Term Expert

  • 1996, 1998, 2002: Polish-Japanese Institute of Information Technology.

Part-Time Lectureships

  • 1989–1999: Aichi Prefectural University of Fine Arts and Music.
  • 1991: Graduate School of Engineering, Hiroshima University.
  • 1991–2007: College of Arts and Sciences, The University of Tokyo.
  • 2005–2007: School of Media Science, Tokyo University of Technology.
  • 2002–2007: School of Design, Kyushu University.
  • 2007–2012: Graduate School of Design, Kyushu University.
  • 2022–2023: Kobe Design University.
